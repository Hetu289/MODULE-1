Celebrate Rakshabandhan | Festival of Sibling Love

Rakshabandhan is a traditional Indian festival celebrating the sacred bond between brothers and sisters. Sisters tie a decorative thread called Rakhi around their brothers’ wrists, praying for their well-being and prosperity. In return, brothers promise to protect them and shower them with gifts. Steps to Celebrate Rakshabandhan: 1. Choose a Rakhi: Select a beautiful Rakhi thread for your brother. 2. Tie the Rakhi: Sisters tie the Rakhi on their brother’s wrist with love. 3. Exchange Gifts: Brothers give gifts as a token of love and protection. 4. Share Sweet Treats: Family members enjoy traditional sweets together. 5. Send Wishes: If far away, send Rakhi and greetings digitally to celebrate together.
