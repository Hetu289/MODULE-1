Ballet

Ballet is a graceful classical dance style, emphasizing posture and poise. Perfect for beginners and advanced dancers alike.

Hip-Hop

Hip-Hop is energetic and modern, combining street moves with rhythm and attitude. Great for freestyle expression.

Salsa

Salsa is a lively Latin dance for pairs, focusing on rhythm, coordination, and footwork. Ideal for social dance events.

Jazz

Jazz dance combines improvisation, rhythm, and sharp movements. Fun and expressive, suitable for stage performances.

Contemporary

Contemporary dance blends ballet and modern styles, focusing on fluidity, emotion, and storytelling through movement.

Tap Dance

Tap dance emphasizes rhythm with shoes, combining percussive footwork and performance skills. A perfect show-stopping style.

Bollywood

Bollywood dance is energetic, colorful, and fun, combining Indian classical and modern styles. Ideal for cultural performances.

Dance Competition

Participate in dance competitions to showcase your skills, creativity, and teamwork, gaining experience and recognition.
