Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights, is one of the most celebrated festivals in India. It symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. On this day, people decorate their homes with diyas (lamps), candles, and colorful rangolis.

Families come together to perform Lakshmi Puja, share sweets, and exchange gifts. Fireworks light up the sky, spreading joy and celebration everywhere. The festival reminds us to shine brightly with positivity and kindness.
