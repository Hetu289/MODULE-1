Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights, is one of the most important Hindu festivals. It symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil, celebrated with joy and togetherness across India.

Traditional Activities During Diwali

  1. Lighting diyas and candles
  2. Decorating homes with rangoli
  3. Performing Lakshmi Puja
  4. Exchanging sweets and gifts
  5. Fireworks and family gatherings

Popular Foods

  • Kaju Katli
  • Laddoos and Barfis
  • Chakli and Namkeen
  • Samosa and festive snacks

Learn more about Diwali celebrations at Wikipedia.

Fun Fact

Diwali is celebrated not only in India but also in countries like Nepal, Sri Lanka, Singapore, and Fiji, bringing people of different cultures together.
