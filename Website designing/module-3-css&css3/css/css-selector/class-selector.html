Hetvi's Dance Tips

  • Always feel the rhythm of the music.
  • Practice makes perfect—dance daily!
  • Be confident in your moves and expressions.
  • Wear comfortable outfits to dance freely.

Favorite Dance Styles

  • Hip Hop – energetic and fun.
  • Contemporary – expressive and emotional.
  • Jazz – stylish and upbeat.
  • Belly Dance – graceful and fluid movements.

Dance Motivation

  • Dance like nobody’s watching.
  • Every step tells a story.
  • Enjoy every beat and rhythm.
  • Hetvi Makasana believes in passion through dance!
