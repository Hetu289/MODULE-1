Level 1 DFD

Entities and Processes:

Patient Module

Input: Patient registration details, appointment request.

Output: Appointment confirmation, medical reports, bills.

Doctor Module

Input: Diagnosis details, prescriptions, test requests.

Output: Patient treatment records, prescriptions.

Admin Module

Input: Staff details, hospital resources, billing data.

Output: Reports, staff schedules, payment receipts.

Database (Data Store)

Stores: Patient records, doctor records, appointments, billing information.
