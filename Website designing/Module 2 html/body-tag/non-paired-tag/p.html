🙏 Ganesh Chaturthi Celebration 🙏

Ganesh Chaturthi is one of the most vibrant and beloved festivals in India, marking the birth of Lord Ganesha — the remover of obstacles and the god of wisdom, prosperity, and good fortune.

Devotees bring beautifully crafted idols of Lord Ganesha to their homes and pandals, offering modaks, flowers, and prayers. Daily aarti is performed with devotion, and the atmosphere is filled with chants of “Ganpati Bappa Morya!”.

Cultural programs, music, and dances add to the festive joy. On the last day, the grand Visarjan (immersion) takes place, where devotees bid farewell to Bappa with love, carrying the spirit of unity, hope, and blessings.
